Glasgow Roller Girls-Home Team Bout

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2011-07-17 11:20 [Source]
Tags:  bad omens death stars grd grg home season tournament upcoming bouts

Glasgow's home teams,the Bad Omens and the Death Stars,go head to head in their first bout this season.The action is at The Arc,Cowcaddens Road,Saturday 30th July,entry £5,under 14's free The Bad Omens are defending champions,so The Death Stars should be hungry to put that right,should make for a great bout!
