Lincolnshire Bombers Roller Girls Vs Glasgow Roller Girls-24/09/11

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2011-09-16 14:09 [Source]
Tags:  grg irn bruisers maiden grrders upcoming bouts

Glasgow Roller Girls travel south to take on the Lincolnshire Bombers Roller Girls in a fantastic double header. First LBRG's Damebusters will play GRG's Maiden Grrders,while later the main event will be between LBRG's Bombshell Bruisers against GRG's Irn Bruisers.This all takes place at the George Stephenson Pavilion,Newark,Saturday the 24th of September,entry £7,under 12's go free.Plus all the usual stalls,raffle and half time entertainment. This will be a superb afternoon of Roller Derby.
