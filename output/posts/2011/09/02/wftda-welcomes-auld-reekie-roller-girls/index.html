WFTDA Welcomes Auld Reekie Roller Girls

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2011-09-02 00:58 [Source]
Tags:  arrg in the news wftda

It's official,the Womens Flat Track Derby Association has welcomed Auld Reekie Roller Girls as a member.Making ARRG Scotland's first full WFTDA member and only the second league in the UK after The London Roller Girls to achieve such a thing. Congratulations to all at ARRG,in particular to those who worked so hard on putting this into place,this is great news for Scottish Derby as a whole.
