Glasgow Roller Girls-Home Team Bout 3-8/10/11

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2011-09-26 11:41 [Source]
Tags:  death stars grg hell's belles upcoming bouts

Glasgow Roller Girls present a Home Team bout on the 8th of October at The Arc in Cowcaddens Road,Glasgow.Entry is £6 on the door,or £5 in advance Under 14's go free. GRG's third home team,The Bad Omens have won their previous games,so have booked their place in the final,this bout will decide who takes them on.
