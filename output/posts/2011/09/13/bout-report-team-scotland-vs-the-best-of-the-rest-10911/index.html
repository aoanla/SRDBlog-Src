Bout Report-Team Scotland Vs The Best Of The Rest 10/9/11

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2011-09-13 11:41 [Source]
Tags:  bout report bout reports pictures team scotland video

Team Scotland played The Best Of The Rest,or "Team Steve" as they were dubbed on the day, at The Arc in Glasgow as part of their fundraising efforts to get the team to the first ever Roller Derby World Cup. You can read an excellent review ,peruse the pictures or watch the video footage and marvel at the sheer volume of the fans.

Many thanks to Aoanla for the work he puts in doing these reviews,the featured pic is also by him,from his blog Ante Ortus A Novus Ars
