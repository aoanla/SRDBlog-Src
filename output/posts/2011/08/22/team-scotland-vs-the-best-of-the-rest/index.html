Team Scotland Vs The Best Of The Rest

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2011-08-22 15:24 [Source]
Tags:  fundraiser event fundraising events roller derby world cup roller derby world cup 2011 team scotland upcoming bouts

On the 10th of September Team Scotland will take on 'The Best Of The Rest' in a fundraising bout at The Arc,Cowcaddens Road,Glasgow.The action starts at 13:30,doors open an hour before and entry is £6,proceeds will go towards helping our skaters get to Toronto for the first Roller Derby World Cup at the end of the year.
