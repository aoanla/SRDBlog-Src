London Rockin' Rollers Vs Auld Reekie Roller Girls

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2011-08-25 13:17 [Source]
Tags:  arrg cannon belles twisted thistles upcoming bouts

ARRG head way down over the border,to take on the London Rockin' Rollers in a fantastic double header... Need For Greed. The first match will be between LRR's Rising Stars and ARRG's Cannon Belles,while the main event will be LRR's All Stars against ARRG's Twisted Thistles.Tickets can be purchased via Paypal at the LRR website
