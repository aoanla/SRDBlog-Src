Glasgow Roller Girls-Home Team Bout 2

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2011-08-16 13:48 [Source]
Tags:  bad omens grd grg hell's belles upcoming bouts

Get along to The Arc,Cowcaddens Road Glasgow for the second of GRG's home team bouts.Kicking off at 12:30pm on the 3rd of September,this bout features Hells Belles vs The Bad Omens.Entry is a paltry £6 or you can get advance tickets for £5 from here as usual,under 14's get in free.
