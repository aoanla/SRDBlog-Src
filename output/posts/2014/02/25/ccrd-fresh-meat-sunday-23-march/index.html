CCRD Fresh Meat - Sunday 23rd March

Posted: by feemccartney 2014-02-25 13:08 [Source]
Tags:  capital city roller derby edinburgh fresh meat men's roller derby

On Sunday 23rd March, Capital City Roller Derby will be holding a Fresh Meat event to recruit new members into their Edinburgh-based Men's Roller Derby League.

Absolutely no skating experience is necessary as the CCRD coaches will teach you basic safety and skating skills. Skating equipment will be provided for on the night, but participants are asked to buy and bring along their own mouth guard.

So if you want to get involved in one of the world's fastest growing sports and have a good time while you're at it contact, please contact newskaters@capitalcityrollerderby.co.uk to reserve your place at this Fresh Meat event.

Further information can be found on CCRD's Facebook Event.
