FVRG vs. Newcastle Roller Girls

Posted: by feemccartney 2014-05-04 19:53 [Source]
Tags:  fierce valley roller girls fvrg stirling the peak upcoming bouts

On Saturday 17th May, Fierce Valley Roller Girls will take on the Whippin' Hinnies from Newcastle Roller Girls at The Peak, in Stirling.

Last year, the Parma Violents travelled to Newcastle to take on the Whippin' Hinnies in a hard-fought bout. A year has passed and this time, it's the turn of the Whippin' Hinnies to come to Stirling for the rematch!

Doors open at 2pm with the first whistle at 2.30pm. Tickets are £5 in advance from Brown Paper Tickets, or £7 on the door. Under 14s go free with a paying adult!

Please see the Facebook Event for more details.
