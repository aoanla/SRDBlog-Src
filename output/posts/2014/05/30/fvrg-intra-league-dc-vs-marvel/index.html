FVRG Intra-League: DC vs. Marvel

Posted: by feemccartney 2014-05-30 12:36 [Source]
Tags:  fierce valley roller girls fvrg grangemouth grangemouth sports complex upcoming bouts

On Sunday 8th June in Grangemouth Sports Complex, skaters from Fierce Valley Roller Girls will become fierce opponents for the day in a comic book themed game!

An intra-league event gives leagues the chance to welcome their newer skaters on track - and this time, Bammy Zuko and Gotham Nicky will be skating alongside past and present Parma Violents.

(L-R) Bammy Zuko & Gotham Nicky

Team Marvel will be playing in white, with Team DC in black - but I bet there will be some kit-customisation on the day as the skaters embrace this colourful theme!

Since Grangemouth Sports Complex is a bit tight for space, it is recommend that you buy your tickets in advance to avoid disappointment. Tickets are priced at £5 online from Brown Paper Tickets, or £7 on the door. Kids under 14 go free with a paying adult.

Doors will open at 3.30pm for a 4pm start. As always, there will be a selection of handmade crafts, home-baking and team merchandise for sale.
