Deadly Divas Roller Derby Meet

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2012-09-18 10:55 [Source]
Tags:  ddrd fresh meat

Image courtesy of DDRD

Deadly Divas Roller Derby are hosting an info night/meet at The Corona Bar,Glasgow, on the 30th of September.Anyone interested in joining Glasgow's newest league to get fit,meet new people and be part of one of the most exciting and fastest growing sports in the world,should pop along to the meet,or register your interest at the Facebook event page. DDRD's first training session takes place soon after on the 6th of October at the Maccabi Sports Centre,Giffnock,more details here.
