Auld Reekie Roller Girls Vs Sheffield Steel Rollergirls

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2012-09-12 12:39 [Source]
Tags:  arrg twisted thistles upcoming bouts

Image courtesy of Auld Reekie Roller Girls

Auld Reekie Roller Girls' Twisted Thistles take on Sheffield Steel Rollergirls at Meadowbank Sports Centre,London Road,Edinburgh on Saturday the 22nd of September.Entry is £7 on the door or £5 plus booking in advance. ARRG won the last time these two teams met at the Chaos On The Clyde tournament,should make for a good bout as SSRG will be hungry to even the score.
