Fair City Rollers Vs Furness Firecrackers

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2012-09-21 23:50 [Source]
Tags:  fcr upcoming bouts

Image courtesy of FCR

Fair City Rollers hold their last home bout of the year against the Furness Firecrackers,at Bell's Sports Centre,Perth.The spy themed action takes place on Saturday the 29th of September,doors open 2pm,entry £6. More details on the Facebook Events page.
