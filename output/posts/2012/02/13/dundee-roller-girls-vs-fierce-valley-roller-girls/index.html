Dundee Roller Girls vs. Fierce Valley Roller Girls

Posted: by feemccartney 2012-02-13 20:01 [Source]
Tags:  drg dundee roller girls fierce valley roller girls fvrg parma violents silvery tayzers upcoming bouts

Fierce Valley Roller Girls' Parma Violents are preparing to take on Dundee Roller Girls' Silvery Tayzers on Saturday 18th February in their first public bout!

'Bruise Brothers' will kick off slightly earlier than usual at 12 noon in The DISC, Dundee. Rest assured, there will be more raffle prizes and cakes than you can shake you tail feather at!

Advanced tickets are available online for £5, or £7 on the door.
