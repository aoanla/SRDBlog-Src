Glasgow Roller Derby Vs Stockholm-Double Header

Posted: by scottishrollerderby 2012-04-08 00:12 [Source]
Tags:  grd irn bruisers maiden grrders upcoming bouts

Image courtesy of Glasgow Roller Derby

Glasgow Roller Derby host a double-header against Stockholm Roller Derby at The Arc,Cowcaddens Road,Glasgow on Saturday the 14th of April.Entry is £8 on the day or £7 in advance,under 14's go free.Doors open at midday. First up will be the Maiden Grrders against Stockholm B,followed by the Irn Bruisers versus Stockholm A,this is bound to be a busy one,so get an advance ticket if you can to avoid disappointment.
