Non-US/Canadian Derby Roundup: 9th/10th December Edition

Posted: by aoanla 2017-12-05 19:32 [Source]
Tags:  articles british roller derby championships international derby international roller derby upcoming bouts upcoming events

We’re bringing you another roundup of the upcoming Roller Derby this weekend from across the world. As always, we’re using Flat Track Stats for much of this information – for regions with less good coverage, we also do a bit of intensive searching using a tool which scans the Facebook events of Roller Derby leagues known to Derby Listing.

Predictions of bouts are from FTS, if possible, and from our own SRD Rank where FTS cannot make predictions (for example: Latin America, or non-MRDA men’s bouts).  (SRDRank also has recent WFTDA rankings, including the 31st June ranking, as well as SRDRank, and allows you to make predictions from them.)

If we’ve missed you from our roundup, please let us know! [Or add yourselves to FTS and/or Derbylisting]

Scotland

This weekend, there's no bouts in Scotland, but:

  • Friday, Dundee: Dundee Roller Girls' Open Scrims are open this time to anyone covered by the WFTDA Gender policy (women and non-binary and gender-expansive people) [EVENT]
  • Dundee: Glasgow Men's Roller Derby host the 5th of their Jammer Club jamming-focussed mini-bootcamp sessions. [EVENT]
  • Edinburgh: Auld Reekie Roller Girls host their annual Snowball celebrating another year of derby for the team and the community around them [EVENT]

UK

With December beginning, there's still derby happening, but many bouts are Christmas themed...:

  • Friday, Oldham, England: Rainy City Roller Derby host another of their New Skater Nights [EVENT]
  • Culm, England: South West Angels of Terror host a super scrim event, open to all skaters (despite the problematic language, this event is Open To All) [EVENT]
  • Manchester, England: Manchester Roller Derby are hosting a Christmas Roller Disco [EVENT]
  • Nottingham, England: the Hellfire Harlots are holding a Christmas Craft Fair at Rough Trade Nottingham [EVENT]
  • Oldham, England: Rainy City Roller Derby host an intraleague double header, with mixed teams from across their 4 travel teams [EVENT]
  • Wolverhampton, England: Wolverhampton Honor Rollers are hosting "The Fightmare before Christmas", a double-header event [EVENT]
    • Open mixed scrim: Jack Skellington versus Oogie Boogie
    • Wolverhampton Honor Rollers versus Riot City Ravens (Newport) [FTS]
  • Sunday, Coventry, England: Coventry Roller Derby host a festive double header [EVENT]
    • Rookie game: Seasons Beatings versus Stocking Killers
    • Coventry Roller Derby + Friends versus Team Unicorn Roller Derby
  • Sunday, Coventry, England: Coventry Roller Derby also host their monthly "Coed" (by which they mean Open to All, despite the problematic wording) Scrimmage [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Grimsby, England: Grimsby Roller Derby host a Christmas Scrim double-header (mixed teams), with a Gremlins theme [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Northampton, England: the Vendetta Vixens host a Christmas Scrim ("female-identifying" only, sadly) [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Wallasey, England: Wirral Pack Animals play against The World in another end-of-season mashup scrimmage [EVENT]
  • Monday, Birmingham, England: the Crash Test Brummies are hosting a Christmas Scrim (which is Open To All, despite the problematic description) [EVENT]

Pacific

In Australia, New Zealand, and Japan it's relatively quiet:

  • Friday, Gladstone, QLD, Australia: Gladstone PCYC hosts their Xmas Roller Disco, complete with a raffle for a Scooter! [EVENT]
  • Gold Coast, QLD, Australia: East Coast Derby Dolls host their Christmas themed bout, mixed teams of Queensland based skaters competing as "Sleigh-her" and "Jingle Belles" [EVENT]
  • Newcastle, NSW, Australia: Newcastle Roller Derby hosts the traditional Team Australia versus Wizards of Aus bout, as the men's and women's national teams face off [FTS][EVENT]
  • Okinawa, Japan: Okinawa Roller Derby host a bout themed around the most important winter tradition: the next Star Wars film! The Resistance will be taking on the First Order on track, with skaters from ORD and Devil Dog Derby Dames [EVENT]
  • Auckland, New Zealand: Pirate City Rollers host their final game - and double header of the year with two home teams bouts [EVENT]
    • Elves versus Reindeers (Junior)
    • Unicorns versus Dinosaurs
  • Sunday, Coffs Harbor, NSW, Australia: Coffs Coast Derby Dolls are seeing off the year with a "five-a-side Christmas Scrim", four teams, playing 15 minute games with no time-outs! [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Yokosuka, Japan: Yokosuka Sushi Rollers host the Seasons Beatings Mashup tournament, with skaters from across Tokyo Roller Derby (and anyone else welcome) [EVENT]
