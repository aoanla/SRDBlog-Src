Non-US/Canadian Derby Roundup: 16th/17th December Edition

Posted: by aoanla 2017-12-12 15:19 [Source]
Tags:  articles british roller derby championships international derby international roller derby upcoming bouts upcoming events

We’re bringing you another roundup of the upcoming Roller Derby this weekend from across the world. As always, we’re using Flat Track Stats for much of this information – for regions with less good coverage, we also do a bit of intensive searching using a tool which scans the Facebook events of Roller Derby leagues known to Derby Listing.

Predictions of bouts are from FTS, if possible, and from our own SRD Rank where FTS cannot make predictions (for example: Latin America, or non-MRDA men’s bouts).  (SRDRank also has recent WFTDA rankings, including the 31st June ranking, as well as SRDRank, and allows you to make predictions from them.)

If we’ve missed you from our roundup, please let us know! [Or add yourselves to FTS and/or Derbylisting]

Scotland

This weekend, there's no bouts in Scotland, but:

  • Friday, Inverness: Inverness City Roller Derby invite you to their Christmas Night Out, with a Christmas themed quiz, and party games. [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Glasgow: Mean City Roller Derby host an open morning scrimmage session for anyone who is interested (including NSOs and Referees) [EVENT]

UK

With December in full sway, there's still derby happening, but many bouts are Christmas themed...:

  • Friday, Oldham, England: Rainy City Roller Derby host another of their regular New Skater Nights [EVENT]
  • Friday, Swindon, England: Wiltshire Roller Derby host a "Women's, Men's and Coed" Christmas Scrim. This follows their usual rule of changing gender policy for each jam - the language used is unclear as to if they mean "WFTDA policy" by "Women's" and so on. Caveat Emptor. [EVENT]
  • Mansfield, England: Mansfield Roller Derby host a mixed intraleague scrim, as Mansfield plays Mansfield. [EVENT]
  • Nottingham, England: Nottingham Roller Derby host a Festive Triple Header [EVENT]
    • Nottingham Roller Girls B versus Hellfire Harlots B (Nottingham) [FTS]
    • Nottingham Roller Girls A versus Lincolnshire Bomber Roller Girls [FTS]
    • Super Smash Brollers (Nottingham M) versus Gothenburg Salty Seamen [FTS]
  • Wales: it's possible that South Wales Silures host Paris' Panam Squad for a closed, MRDA-Sanctioned bout, according to FTS. [FTS]
  • Sunday, Leicester, England: Leicestershire Dolly Rockit Rollers host their traditional Propeh Scrimbo scrimmage, for mid/advanced level skaters [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Lincoln, England: Lincolnshire Bombers Roller Girls host their annual Christmas Cherry Popper, an open-subscription rookie bout for skaters with <4 games' experience. [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Manchester, England: Manchester Roller Derby host their final scrimmage of the year - a mixed-level open, all-gender (thanks to MRD for their thoughtful use of language here) scrim. [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Middlesbrough, England: Middlesbrough Roller Derby host an open scrimmage with WFTDA-gender-policy rules (thanks to MRD for being thoughtful about expressing this) [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Norwich, England: Norfolk Roller Derby host an Xmas Intraleague double header [EVENT]
    • "Women's only" (we assume WFTDA-gender-policy...) intraleague
    • "Coed" (Open To All) intraleague
  • Sunday, York, England: York Minxters host their open, WFTDA-gender-policy Christmas Scrim [EVENT]
  • Sunday, Swansea, Wales: Swansea City Roller Derby host a Crimbo Bash Up Scrim (policy not mentioned) [EVENT]
