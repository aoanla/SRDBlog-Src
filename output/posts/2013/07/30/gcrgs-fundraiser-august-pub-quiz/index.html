The Granite City Roller Girls are looking for your help to raise funds!

Friday 2nd August, 7pm

GCRG's Pub Quiz August 2013

Get your head and the books and get back to school with Miss Mitchell and Miss Pert! It's time for your reassessments!

Pub Quiz, Prizes (cold, hard ca$h!), General Shenanigans!

Doors to G2 (up stairs in the Garage via the Campus) will open at 7, with the test, ahem, Quiz starting at 7:30pm sharp!

Teams of no more than 5. £5 entry per person, per team. All proceeds go to your favourite Aberdeen based Roller Derby team, GCRG.
