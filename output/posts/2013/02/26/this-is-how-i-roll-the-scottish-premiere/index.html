'This Is How I Roll' - The Scottish Premiere

Posted: by feemccartney 2013-02-26 13:07 [Source]
Tags:  fundraising events mean city roller derby

Mean City Roller Derby are proud to present the Scottish Premiere of This Is How I Roll! The movie screening will be shown at Maryhill Hub, Glasgow on Saturday 2nd March from 7pm.

[youtube http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lCEPJnacDw0]

Tickets are available on the door and priced as follows: Adult VIP - £12.50 (Includes Entry, MCRD T-Shirt and Sticker); Adult - £5; Child (under 16's): £2.50. Capacity at the venue is limited, but you can reserve a place directly from MCRD via their Facebook Event.
