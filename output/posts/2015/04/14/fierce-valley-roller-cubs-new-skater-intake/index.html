Fierce Valley Roller Cubs New Skater Intake

Posted: by camelondiaz 2015-04-14 12:37 [Source]
Tags:  fierce valley roller cubs fresh meat junior roller derby

Fierce Valley Roller Cubs are recruiting! The next new skater intake will be held on Sunday 26th April 2015, at the Mariner Centre, Falkirk. FVRC welcomes girls and boys aged 5-17, with no skating experience necessary, to come along and give it a try. Email freshmeat@fiercevalleyrollercubs.co.uk for more information and to register your interest.
