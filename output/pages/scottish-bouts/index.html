Scottish Bouts

It can be quite hard to know what bouts are coming up, what with different leagues all having their own ways of getting the word out.

This page aims to collate all of the upcoming bouts we're aware of at the Scottish Roller Derby blog, in Scotland, so you don't miss anything.

There's been something of a hiatus on this page for a while. (We simply didn't have the time to track all the bouts this year, what with Champs and all that.) However, Capital City Roller Derby are working on their own list, here, which might help.
